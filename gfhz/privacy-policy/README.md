## Privacy Policy

### Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to 规范汉字 (the "App").

The App is free to use and does not offer paid subscriptions or in-app purchases.

### Information We Collect

We do not collect, upload, or store any personally identifiable user data.

The App does not require account registration and does not collect personal information such as your name, phone number, email address, contacts, location, communications, or browsing history.

### Permissions and On-Device Processing

The App may request the following system permissions only when you actively use related features:

- Camera access: to capture images for text recognition.
- Photo library access: to select images for text recognition.

Images and recognized text are processed for App features and are not uploaded to our servers.

### Local History Storage

To provide history features, the App stores the following data locally on your device:

- Characters you have viewed
- Search text you have entered
- Text recognized from images

This data remains on your device only, is not synced to our servers, and is not shared with third parties. You can clear history within the App.

### Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

The App does not use third-party advertising SDKs, does not perform cross-app tracking, and does not support social login.

### Children's Privacy

The App does not intentionally collect personal information from children. Because we do not collect personal data, we do not process children's personal data.

### Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy when App features or legal requirements change. Any updated version will be posted on this page and becomes effective on the date it is published.
